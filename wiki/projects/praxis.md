# PROJECT: PRAXIS
> Social OS for goal tracking & accountability.

---

## OVERVIEW
Praxis is a multi-platform social ecosystem designed for coordinate human action without controlling motive.

## VALIDITY LAYERS
1. **Self-Honesty:** Periodic belief checks.
2. **Collaborative Validation:** Maturity tracking from Personal to Validated.
3. **Reality Anchoring:** Grade-outcome divergence detection.

## COMPONENTS
- **WebApp:** React/TS (`~/Praxis/praxis_webapp`).
- **Desktop:** Electron (`~/Praxis/praxis_desktop`).
- **Linux:** Native build (`~/Praxis/praxis_linux`).
